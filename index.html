What Do We Do?

At Community Assist, we connect sports teams with families in need during the Christmas season. Our mission is to ensure every family can celebrate the holidays with gifts and essentials that they might not otherwise have access to. By partnering with us, sports teams have the unique opportunity to make a direct and lasting impact on their communities. Join us in spreading joy and making a difference. Together, we can make this holiday season truly special for everyone.

We encourage sports teams to step forward and lend a helping hand. Your team's support can light up lives and create memories that will last a lifetime. Together, let's build stronger, more compassionate communities!
